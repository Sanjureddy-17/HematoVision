HematoVision

Advanced Blood Cell Classification Using Transfer Learning

About the Application

HematoVision is an artificial intelligence–based web application designed to classify human blood cells using deep learning and transfer learning techniques. The system analyzes microscopic blood cell images and identifies the cell type with high accuracy.

By leveraging pre-trained convolutional neural networks, HematoVision reduces manual effort in blood cell analysis and assists healthcare professionals, researchers, and students in understanding blood cell morphology.

Upload Blood Cell Image

Please upload a microscopic image of a blood cell. The model will analyze the image and predict whether the cell is an eosinophil, lymphocyte, monocyte, or neutrophil.
